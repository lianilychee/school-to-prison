Suspension Rates and School Demographics

Data regarding school discipline rates and general demographic information per school district was sourced from UCLA's Center for Civil Rights Remedies' School Discipline Data project. Data reported from this set are from the 2012 Secondary School District report. Download the complete dataset here.

Arrest Rates

Data regarding juvenile and overall arrest rates was provided by the U.S. Office of Juvenile Justice and Delinquency Prevention. The dataset is not readily downloadable, but is accessible by-county here. Data reported is from the year 2012.

Poverty Rates

Data regarding poverty information is provided by the U.S. Census Bureau's Small Area Income & Poverty Estimates (SAIPE). The dataset used for this project was the 2013 School District Estimates. The dataset is available here. Though SAIPE cautions comparing across districts, it does so mostly for serious statistical hypothesis validation. Its use on this project is appropriate, as it is intended to contextualize regional data.
